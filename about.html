your new favorite coffee spot

Classic New York restaurant culture meets authentic Italian coffee here at Affogato Coffee Club.

our history

Named for our signature Italian dessert, Affogato Coffee Club was born out of the love of NYC restaurant culture, and a desire to bring authentic Italian coffee and pastries to the forefront of our city's coffee scene. Though we just opened this year, we're excited to become part of NYC's vibrant community of small businesses.

our philosophy

Our goal is to create a welcoming, warm, accepting space for everyone to experience a cozy and safe environment with a hot cup of coffee. Through our partnerships with local non-profits, we believe in loving our community first, and that our goal will always be, first and foremost, to care for each other.
